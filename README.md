## Planetory Saviours

Spare some time of your busy day-to-day schedule and try 
this app and take a pledge with us to save this 
beautiful world at any cost. Don't you worry, its 
not as hard as saving the country at the border but 
yes you can adjust your daily habits to add your 
contribution towards saving the world 
and become a member of The Planetery Saviours.

![image](https://github.com/RitickSaha/Hack20/blob/master/screenshots/Screenshot_20200629_162111_com.example.flutterappearth.jpg)

Features of Application:

1. Provides amazing facts about our earth and environment which people 
   rarely know about. After knowing these facts, you will know your 
   earth better and the better you understand the more you come close
   to it and the more you care for it.

   ![image](https://github.com/RitickSaha/Hack20/blob/master/screenshots/Screenshot_20200629_162115_com.example.flutterappearth.jpg)

2. Suggests 5 new affordable tasks everyday. you may think these tasks
   rubbish but these small tasks produce huge impact on global level.
   Choose those tasks which you can afford and try to complete them 
   within the time period specified and get rewarded with our exclusive
   badges. you may not earn a single penny but when you broad you spectrum
   of vision, you will realise that this small contribution led to millions
   creature of this world.

   ![image](https://github.com/RitickSaha/Hack20/blob/master/screenshots/Screenshot_20200629_162123_com.example.flutterappearth.jpg)

3. View your earth emotions, now you don't need to watch NASA forecast to 
   see your earth. we brought it to your device through this app that too 
   with enabled emotions. For example, lets consider two scenario:
     ** a) Jan 2020: Everything was normal but earth was crying so for sad 
                  emotion we added red theme to earth **
     ** b) May 2020: Everything is abnormal but earth is recovering and for 
		  happy state we added green color **
   We added few more emotions which we won't mention here, find it yourself
   by exploring the app

   ![image](https://github.com/RitickSaha/Hack20/blob/master/screenshots/Screenshot_20200629_162126_com.example.flutterappearth.jpg)

4. Tree Plantation Stats: We added a section where you can see the current feeds
   of total no of trees grown worldwide everyday. these statistics 
   are fetched from a very authentic website xyz.com. so you can be sure of the 
   data. moreover you can see the individual contributions of the Planetory Tycoons
   too. just click on the "View detailed Stats" button.

   ![image](https://github.com/RitickSaha/Hack20/blob/master/screenshots/SVID-20200629-162136-1.gif)

At last we want to say that to become a hero, you don't need to wear a cap. Just 
keep calm and before doing some harm to the environment, just think once that is 
it necessary. Do as much tasks you can and become the Super Saviour of this world.
